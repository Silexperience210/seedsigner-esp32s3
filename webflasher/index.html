SeedSigner ESP32-S3

Air-gapped Bitcoin signing device

Version v0.2.1

⚡ Features

  • BIP39 Seed generation & backup
  • XPUB/YPUB/ZPUB export
  • PSBT signing
  • Address verification
  • QR code display & scanning
  • Built for M5Stack CoreS3
⚠️ Important: Use Chrome, Edge, or Opera browser. Connect your ESP32-S3 via USB and click "Connect" to flash.
Use Chrome, Edge, or Opera browser

📋 Instructions

  1. Connect your M5Stack CoreS3 to USB
  2. Click "Connect & Install" above
  3. Select the COM port in the popup
  4. Wait for the flash to complete (~30 seconds)
  5. Device will restart automatically
